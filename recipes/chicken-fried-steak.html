Chicken Fried Steak

Chicken Fried Steak is a meal consisting of a steak (often cubed steak), that is battered and fried. Thought to have origionated from Austria or Germany in the 1800s the dish made its way to Texas where it has spread to become a staple Southern dish.

Ingredients

  • Cubed Steaks
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Saltine Crackers
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cover cubed steaks in buttermilk and let rest for 1 hour or up to overnight
  2. Mix together 2 handfulls of flour, sprink salt and pepper in
  3. Lightly crush up crackers into coarse bits, add to flour mix
  4. One at a time place cubed steaks into dry mix and press flour and crackers into steaks
  5. Fry one or two steaks at a time at 375F for 3-5 minutes each side or until golden brown
