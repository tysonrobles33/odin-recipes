Fried Rice

Fried rice is a versatile dish served across much of Asia. It often consists of any protein of choice along with vegetables mix and egg

Ingredients

  • Rice(Best if a day old)
  • Vegetable mix
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Seasame Seed Oil
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Heat up butter in a very hot saucepan
  2. Place rice in butter and begin to fry, keeping rice moving
  3. Once the rice starts to brown add in seasame oil and soy sauce to taste
  4. Allow the rice to fry a little more while adding in vegetable mix
  5. Make a well in the center of the pan and add in a little more butter
  6. Add 2-4 beaten eggs to the well, and stir constantly
  7. When the eggs begin to cook mix everything together and cook until done
  8. Optionally add back in any protein to the pan and serve with Sriracha
